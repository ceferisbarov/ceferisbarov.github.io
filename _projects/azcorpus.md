AzCorpus is built for text generation purposes. It contains a total of 1.9 million documents, drawn from a variety of sources. The corpus is designed to provide a broad range of linguistic data for natural language processing and organized by genre and topic, with texts covering a range of subjects including politics, economics, science, culture, sport, history, society and etc.
  
Website: [Detexter](https://huggingface.co/datasets/azcorpus/azcorpus_v0)
