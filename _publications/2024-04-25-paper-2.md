Spelling correction in NLP, especially for agglutinative languages like Azerbaijani, remains challenging due to their rich morphology. Previous attempts using neural networks, such as autoregressive encoder-decoder models, suffer from uncertain predictions, impacting their reliability for production systems. In this study, we address this uncertainty issue by modifying the architecture to enhance prediction robustness. Methods evaluated include deep ensemble techniques, with comparative analysis conducted.
